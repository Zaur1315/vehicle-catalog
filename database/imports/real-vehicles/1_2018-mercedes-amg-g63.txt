## Overview

This 2018 Mercedes-AMG G63 is finished in **Designo Night Black Magno** over a **Classic Red and black interior** and represents one of the later examples of the original W463-generation AMG G-Wagen before the major redesign that followed. Combining the iconic boxy G-Class styling with AMG performance, this G63 offers luxury SUV comfort, serious off-road hardware, and strong twin-turbo V8 power in one package.

Power comes from a **5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8** paired with a **7-speed automatic transmission** and a **2-speed transfer case**. In stock form, this engine is rated at **563 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque**, though this example has been modified with **Fi Exhaust catless downpipes**, an **active exhaust system**, and a **RENNtech ECU tune**, so output may be higher than factory specification.
