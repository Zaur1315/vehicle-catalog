## Overview

This 2020 Ford F-150 Harley-Davidson Edition 4x4 is finished in **Lead Foot** over a **black interior** and shows approximately **10,100 miles**. Built by **Tuscany Motor Company** on the F-150 platform, this special-edition truck combines the equipment and comfort of a well-optioned Lariat with a long list of Harley-Davidson-themed styling, suspension, and performance upgrades.

Power comes from a **supercharged 5.0-liter V8** paired with a **10-speed automatic transmission** and a **4-wheel-drive system**. In factory naturally aspirated form, Ford rated the 5.0-liter V8 at **395 horsepower and 400 lb-ft of torque**, though this truck has been upgraded with a supercharger and supporting performance components. Seller comments in the listing indicate the Tuscany build sheet references **700+ horsepower**, though no dyno sheet was provided.

The attached vehicle history report shows **no accidents or mileage discrepancies**. According to the window sticker shown in the gallery, factory equipment includes **Equipment Group 502A**, the **Technology Package**, the **Trailer Tow Package**, a **3.73 electronic-locking rear axle**, a **twin-panel sunroof**, and **adaptive cruise control**. Tuscany/Harley-Davidson conversion equipment adds a wide range of mechanical, exterior, and interior upgrades.
